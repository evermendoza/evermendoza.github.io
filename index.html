I'm Ever Mendoza — a communications professional and digital builder based in Atlanta, GA.

I grew up between two worlds: Paraguay, where I was born and built my career, and Atlanta, where I went to high school and always knew I'd return. That back-and-forth shaped how I think — I understand both markets, both cultures, and both languages from the inside.

Over the past 10+ years I've built websites, managed brands, run social media campaigns, and helped businesses across Latin America establish a real digital presence. I've worked with local shops, agencies, and professionals who needed someone who could think strategically and execute practically — without the agency price tag.

I hold a Bachelor's in Public Relations and I'm finishing a second degree in Computer Systems Analysis. I believe communication and technology are the same discipline now — and the people who understand both are the ones who actually get things done.

Estoy en Atlanta, listo para ayudarte. Si tu negocio necesita presencia digital, contenido bilingüe o una estrategia que realmente funcione, comunícate hoy mismo.
